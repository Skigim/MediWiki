# Adult AD Waiver Initial Determination Process# Adult AD Waiver Initial Determination Process

*Streamlined Interdivisional Procedures for Disability and Level of Care Evaluation*



## Overview---



This comprehensive guide outlines the interdivisional processes across MLTC (Division of Medicaid & Long-Term Care) and DDD (Division of Developmental Disabilities) for initial determinations of disability and level of care within the context of Medicaid eligibility for adults age 18 or older requesting Aged and Disabled (AD) Waiver services. The process is designed to achieve timely, efficient, customer-focused results.

---

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2023  

**Scope:** Applies to initial Medicaid applications for individuals age 18+ requesting AD Waiver services
